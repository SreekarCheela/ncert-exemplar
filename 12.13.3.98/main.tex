\textbf{Question:} If A and B are two independent events then $\pr{AB} = \pr{A}.\pr{B}$

\textbf{Solution:} Two events A and B are said to be independent if probabilitity of one event occuring 
does not depend on the ocurring of the other event.

Hence we can say;
\begin{align}
    \pr{A|B} = \pr{A}
\end{align}
but we also know that;
\begin{align}
    \pr{A|B} = \frac{\pr{AB}}{\pr{B}}
\end{align}
Hence from (1) and (2) we can say that;
\begin{align}
    \pr{A} = \frac{\pr{AB}}{\pr{B}}\\
    \pr{A}.\pr{B} = \pr{AB}
\end{align}
Hence proved.
